Strawberry mug cake

Description

Sweet and flavourful strawberry cake in a mug.

Takes 5 minutes in total for cooking and prep.

Serves 1 person.

Ingredients
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1 1/2 tbsp cane sugar
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 3 tbsp milk (preferably strawberry milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • About 1/8 teaspoon salt (A pinch)
  • 3-4 diced strawberries

Optional:

  • Red or pink food dye
  • Whipped cream
  • Chocolate chips
Preparation directions
  1. In a large mug, add coconut oil, milk, vanilla extract, and sugar. If you want, add a few drops of food dye as well.
  2. Add the flour and the baking powder to the mug by stirring in small amounts. Make sure the mixture is thoroughly mixed with no batter on the sides of the mug or dry ingredients at the bottom.
  3. Add the diced strrawberries and carefully mix into the batter. Save some to sprinkle on top.
  4. Put the mug in the microwave and cook it on high for 1 minute and 30 seconds. If the batter is still wet, microwave in 10 second intervals until a toothpick comes out clean.
  5. Allow the mug cake to rest for about half a minute. Then, top with whipped cream, chocolate chips, or more strawberries!
