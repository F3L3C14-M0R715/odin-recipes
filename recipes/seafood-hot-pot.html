Spicy Seafood seafood hotpot

Description

Spicy and flavourful Hot Pot.

Takes 40 minutes in total for cooking and prep.

Serves 4 people.

Ingredients

Solid contents

  • 8 mussels
  • 2 blue crabs
  • 8 ounces of squid or octopus
  • 8 ounces white fish like tilapia
  • 8 large shrimp (shell on)
  • 6 ounces of soybean sprouts
  • 8 ounces napa cabbage
  • 8 ounces of napa cabbage (the innermost parts)
  • 3 ounces of Korean radish
  • 1 medium carrot
  • 1/2 medium onion
  • 3 mushroom caps
  • 2 scallions
  • 2 ounces of watercress

Broth

  • 3 1/2 cups of Anchovy Broth
  • 1 tbsp of lemon juice

Seasoning

  • 3 tbsp of gochugaru
  • 1 tbsp soup soy sauce or fish sauce
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp gochujang
  • 1 tsp doenjang
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
Preparation directions
  1. Scrub the mussles and place them in salted water for 30 minutes to eject any sand.
  2. Serparate the top shells from the crabs and cut out the gills. Clean the shells with a spare toothbrush. Break/cut the bodies into halves.
  3. Clean the squid and fish, cut into bite size pieces.
  4. Clean the shrimp.
  5. Cut the cabbage, onion, mushrooms, radish, and carrot into thin, bite-size pieces.
  6. Mix all seasoning ingredients together with 3 tbsp of broth.
  7. Using a shallow pot, arrange all vegetables except the watercress in neat clusters and scatter the seafood pieces on top with the mussels at the bottom.
  8. Add 3 cups of broth and all the seasoning paste and top with the watercress.
  9. Cook over medium high heat until the mussels are open, the fish is cooked, and the vegetables are soft. Drizzle with the lemon juice.
