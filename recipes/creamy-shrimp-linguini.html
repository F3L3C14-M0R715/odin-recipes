Creamy Garlic Linguini with Shrimp

Description

Hearty linguine pasta covered in a rich,garlicy sauce with garlic-imbued shrimp.

Ingredients
  • 2 tbsp salt + some as a condiment
  • Pepper
  • 220 grams of linguine
  • 15 peeled shrimp
  • 10 cloves of garlic (unpeeled)
  • 2 tbsp of olive oil
  • 1 1/2 cups of whipping cream
  • 1/2 cup of Parmesan cheese + some on the side for topping
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped parsley and chives. Or herbs of choice.
Preparation directions
  1. Boil a large pot of water and add the 2 tbsp of salt to it.
  2. Once the water has begun to boil, add the linguine and let it cook for about 2-3 minutes stirring occaisionally.
  3. On another burner, boil 6 cups of water in a large saucepan.
  4. Drain the pasta and set it aside.
  5. Skip this step if your shrimp is bought previously deveined.Take a small knife and run it under the vein visible along the back of each shrimp. Afterwards, rinse them under running water. Set aside.
  6. To the boiling water in the saucepan, add the cloves of garlic and let it cook for 2 minutes.
  7. Once the garlic has sat in the boiling water for 2 minutes, toss in the shrimp and let it boil with the garlic for 3 more minutes.
  8. Drain the shrimp and garlic and seperate them. Peel and chop the garlic finely. Set both aside.
  9. Set a burner to medium heat with a large skillet over it. Add the olive oil. Then, add the garlic and let it cook for 1 minute.
  10. To the skillet, add the whipping cream along with the garlic and bring it to a simmer. Stir occasionally until the cream thickens. Stir in the Parmesan,herbs, and shrimp. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  11. Place the linguine in a large bowl and pour the sauce and shrimp over it. Toss it to coat evenly. Afterwards, plate and garnish with more Parmesan and herbs.
